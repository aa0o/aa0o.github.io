wap wanderlust

This is a group start up project that my friends and I are working on for a compitition in Germany.I am involved in the production of the content for the service, along with managing and assisting in development and social media activities. In addition to that, I am also responsible for any communications in Germany as I was born and raised there I am a fluent German speaker.

Wanderlust is a companion app for travelers, the main functionality of the application is the mapping and location features which promote local landmarks, recommended restaurants and various other attractions such as recommended hostels/hotels. The majority of such content will be user-generated and user updated, meaning only verification of data will need to come from the company itself.
